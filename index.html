Hi, I’m Shazin, a passionate web developer specializing in creating enriching front end experiences, though I often find myself intrigued by backend technologies and how both applications are used in conjunction to build the world wide web.

The online world and its endless possibilities has always fascinated me.

One day, still trying to figure out what I truly wanted to pursue as a career path, I realized that I mainly wanted a way to make money, so I Googled it, “How to make money online”. I never thought one search would change my life forever.

The core of making money online at the time was through a website. After researching and discovering how websites are created using code, my interest peaked and I realized this was something I was genuinely interested in learning more about. From there, I furthered my education as a Webmaster and combined my experience in the fine arts, print/digital marketing and background in writing, along with my natural flare for creativity, and pursued a path in front end development.

I love the internet and have dedicated myself to being a part of those infinite possibilities.
